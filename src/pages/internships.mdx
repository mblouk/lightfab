We are currently inviting researchers and faculty to submit project proposals for our collaborative training internships program. 

The program will fund five Master’s-level internships (five to six months each) for the 2026-2027 academic year. To promote true cross-collaboration, every funded project must be **jointly supervised** across two different disciplines or institutions. Where possible, we also highly encourage involving an industrial partner. 

*Please note: This current call is strictly for selecting the internship topics. Once a project is approved and funded, supervisors will be responsible for recruiting their own M2 student.*

### Eligibility
* **Target Level:** The proposed research project must be appropriately scoped for a student entering their second year of a Master's program (M2) or an equivalent final-year engineering degree. 
* **Supervision Requirement:** Proposals must explicitly demonstrate co-supervision between at least two distinct disciplines or institutions. 

### Important Dates
* <Badge type="success" isPill>Open</Badge> **Call opening:** June 15, 2026
* **Submission deadline:** October 2, 2026
* **Announcement of the selection decisions:** October 16, 2026
* **Start of the internship:** From January 2027

### How to Submit
To facilitate the review process, please submit your proposal as a **single, integrated PDF document (2-pages maximum)**.

Your PDF should clearly outline:
* **The Research Topic:** A comprehensive description of the scientific project and its objectives.
* **The Interdisciplinary Approach:** An explanation of how the project bridges your two different fields or institutions, including the specific roles and complementary expertise of each co-supervisor.
* **Industrial Partnership (Optional):** Details regarding the involvement of any industry partners, if applicable.

**Submit your project proposal** via email to: lightfab-2027@univ-cotedazur.fr
