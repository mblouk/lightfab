The program will offer four awards to support visits from external established researchers/professors. The visits will have a maximum duration of one month. Each fellow will receive approx €2,500 per month for travel and living expenses. The program aims to foster collaboration between UniCA and external research groups while providing young researchers with the opportunity to engage with the LIGHTFAB network and its facilities.

### Important dates
A call for applications will be launched in early september, with selection decisions announced in early November 2026.

### Young researchers visits (PhD, postdocs)
<Badge type="info" isPill>Info</Badge> The PhD/postdoc are not eligible to this call but can apply to the dedicated Complex Academy call (check dates). If you're interested, the Lightfab board can provide a recommendation letter to support your application.
